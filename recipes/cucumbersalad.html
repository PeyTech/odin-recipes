Nina's cucumber salad

Steps:

Mix sour cream, lemon juice, and dill together in a bowl.

Add cucumbers and onion; stir to combine. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fersh dill
  • 3 cucumbers, halved then sliced thinly
  • 1/2 cup thinly sliced red onion
  • coarse salt and ground black pepper
